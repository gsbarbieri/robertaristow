About

Roberta Ristow is an art consultant and journalist based in Lisbon, working across Brazil and Europe. She specialized in Art Management at Sotheby’s Institute of Art in London, where she lived for two years.

Her professional practice spans cultural management, strategic communication, and art advisory. She collaborates with institutions, collectors, and artists on exhibitions, cultural initiatives, and content-driven projects connecting art, education, and public engagement.

Since 2020, Roberta has been Institutional Director of Casa da Escada Colorida, an independent, non-profit art space in Rio de Janeiro dedicated to artistic research and experimentation. As a journalist, she has contributed to Vogue Brasil since 2017, writing on contemporary art and culture.

Previously, she led the Branded Content department at Editora Globo / Grupo Globo for nine years, developing multiplatform projects for more than 35 international brands and working closely with TV Globo and Globo Condé Nast.

She believes, as Louise Bourgeois once said, that “art is a guarantee of sanity” — and remains central to her professional practice.
